2. Get Historical XRates

Input:
URL: {IPFS-HOST}/ipns/{IPFS-ID}/io-hs/data/xrates/ {SOURCE_CURRENCY} /{TARGET_CURRENCY}/{YYYY}/{MM}/{DD}/{HH}/index.json
No Name Details
1 SOURCE_CURRENCY Source Currency
2 TARGET_CURRENCY Target Currency
3 YYYY Year of the Date
4 MM Month of the Date
5 DD Day of the Date
6 HH Hour of the Date
Output:
